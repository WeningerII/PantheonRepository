- **Proper-name figures** (Makunaima, the flood brothers, Kuarahy, Jasy, Ñamandú, etc.): their NAME is the native term for name-bound faculties (eponymous/lineage/identity-spheres).
- **Spheres/offices** (culture-hero, shaman, sun, thunder, flood, etc.): native concept-word, inherit=none.
- **Heritable bodily powers**: partial/full.

**Notes / caveats:**
- WebFetch was fully blocked (HTTP 403 on every host) in this environment, so verification was done via WebSearch against the primary ethnographic record: **Cadogan's *Ayvu Rapyta* (1959)** and **Nimuendajú's *Die Sagen von der Erschaffung und Vernichtung der Welt als Grundlagen der Religion der Apapocúva-Guaraní* (1914)** for Guaraní; **Koch-Grünberg's *Vom Roroima zum Orinoco* (1916–28)**, **Armellada's *Gramática y diccionario de la lengua pemón* (1943)**, and **Thomas, *Order Without Government* (1982)** for Pemón.
- **No em-dashes were needed** — every faculty, including all titles/offices/spheres, has an attested native concept-word. Culture-hero and lineage-ancestor faculties are rendered with the figure's own name (the native term) where the faculty is identity-bound, and with the native sphere-word (piasan, eremu, Pia, tüpü, tuna) otherwise.
- A few Pemón orthographic forms (a-pëtoi "wind", wëk/warükü "tinamou", esaka'ma "transform", yatapïka "fell", anaï "seed", esenumenka "scout/think") follow Armellada's mission orthography; dialect spelling varies across Arekuna/Taurepang/Kamarakoto. The brothers' personal names (Wacalambé, Anzikilán, Zigué, Ma'nápe, Insikiran) are themselves the authentic Pemón/Macuxi terms recorded by Koch-Grünberg and de Cora.
- **Inheritability:** only genuinely bodily/heritable powers are marked partial/full — transformation (esaka'ma/jeapo), felling-strength, animal-camouflage, monster-slaying prowess (partial), and the divine speech/knowledge faculties ñe'ẽ and mba'ekuaa passed father-to-twin (full). All spheres, offices, and name-bound identities are inherit=none.

No new `+` faculty additions were necessary; all 64 existing faculty IDs are covered.
