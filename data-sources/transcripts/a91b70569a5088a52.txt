A few sourcing/judgment notes:
- **Primary-source anchors**: Codrington's *The Melanesians* (1891) for Vanuatu/Banks Islands (Tagaro, Qat, Marawa), and Malinowski's *Argonauts of the Western Pacific* (1922) + *Coral Gardens and their Magic* (1935) + *The Sexual Life of Savages* (1929) for the Trobriand figures — both available as full text (Wikisource; Project Gutenberg/Monoskop). - **Native terms**: these are predominantly oral languages recorded in Latin orthography, so `term.script` is "Latin" and `rom` equals the value. Where I could not verify a specific attested native word for a concept, I left `term.value` empty rather than inventing one (per Hard Rule 1).
- **Tooling note**: `WebFetch` was network-blocked (HTTP 403) for essentially every domain in this sandbox, so I sourced from `WebSearch` result snippets, which returned substantial verbatim ethnographic content.
