- Authored 34 missing figures: the full Trimūrti (Vishnu, Shiva, Brahma), Mahādevī cluster (Devi, Durga, Parvati, Kali, Lakshmi, Sarasvati), Ganesha and Kartikeya (parented to `hindu_shiva`+`hindu_parvati`, both authored here), the Rāmāyaṇa principals (Rama, Sita, Lakshmana, Ravana, Hanuman), Krishna and Balarama, the lokapālas/Vedic deities (Agni, Varuna, Kubera, Chandra/Soma, Yama, Aditi, Prithvi, Ushas, Saranyu, Kashyapa, Vishwakarma, Kamadeva, Nandi, Garuda), and four well-attested Vishnu avatāras explicitly tied to the Daśāvatāra canon (Narasimha, Varaha, Parashurama) plus Saranyu to ground Yama's maternity.
- Parentage is conservative: only `hindu_surya` (Yama), `hindu_vayu` (Hanuman), and the in-batch `hindu_shiva`/`hindu_parvati` (Ganesha, Kartikeya) are asserted, since those are securely attested and resolvable in-corpus. Where canonical parents (Vasudeva/Devaki/Rohini, Daśaratha's queens, Viśravas/Kaikasī, Janaka) are not in the corpus and weren't authored, I left `parentIds: []` per the rules rather than invent ids.
- Every term carries Devanāgarī + IAST; named weapons/mounts (Sudarśana Chakra, Pāñcajanya, Kaumodakī, Śārṅga, Nandaka, Pināka, Triśūla, Pāśupatāstra, Kodaṇḍa, Candrahāsa, Puṣpaka, vel, hala, musala, the various vāhanas, etc.) are itemized in `materialCulture` with citations; abilities are kept distinct from domains as verb-phrase faculties. All claims were checked against Ṛgveda, the epics, and the major Purāṇas via web verification — no modern coinages or fabricated entities.
