I now have thorough, well-attested research on all figures with specific KTU citations, native Ugaritic terms, distinct domains vs. faculties, and the divine-descent ladder. Let me compile the complete registry.

Key facts confirmed across sources:
- **El** (ʾil): supreme creator, "Bull" (ṯr), "Father of years" (ʾab šnm), "Creator of creatures" (bny bnwt), dwells at source of two rivers, head of divine council, decrees authority. Philo: Elus/Cronus.
- **Athirat/Asherah** (ʾaṯrt): "Lady Athirat of the Sea," "creatress of the gods" (qnyt ʾilm), mother of 70 sons, intercedes with El for Baal's palace (KTU 1.4).
- **Baal-Hadad** (bʿl/hd): storm-king, son of Dagan, Mount Zaphon, weapons Yagrush & Ayamur forged by Kothar (KTU 1.2 IV).
- **Anat** (ʿnt): warrior "maiden" (btlt), slays foes, wades in blood (KTU 1.3 II), kills Mot by winnowing/grinding (KTU 1.6 II), seizes Aqhat's bow (KTU 1.18).
- **Mot** (mt): death, son of El, huge mouth/throat, swallows Baal (KTU 1.5), ground like grain (KTU 1.6).
- **Yam** (ym): Prince Sea, Judge River (ṯpṭ nhr), given kingship by El, defeated by Baal (KTU 1.2).
- **Dagan** (dgn): grain, father of Baal, second after El.
- **Athtart/Astarte** (ʿṯtrt): restrains Baal (KTU 1.2 I), "Name of Baal," hexes Yam (KTU 1.2 IV).
- **Shapshu** (špš): sun-goddess, "torch of the gods" (nrt ʾilm), psychopomp, helps Anat carry Baal (KTU 1.6).
- **Yarikh** (yrḫ): moon, "lamp of heaven," weds Nikkal (KTU 1.24).
- **Kothar-wa-Hasis** (kṯr-w-ḫss): smith, dwells Memphis/Caphtor, forges Baal's weapons & Aqhat's bow.
- **Resheph** (ršp): plague/war, bow & arrow, gatekeeper of Shapshu, in Kirta (one-fifth gathered).
- **Kotharot** (kṯrt): seven swallow-goddesses, midwives, KTU 1.24 & 1.17.
- **Kirta** (krt): son of El, demigod, KTU 1.14-1.16.
- **Aqhat** (ʾaqht): son of Daniel, KTU 1.17-1.19.
- **Daniel/Daniʾilu** (dnʾil): sage-king, judge of widow/orphan, one of the Rephaim.
- **Rephaim** (rpum): deified dead kings, KTU 1.20-1.22.

Report — what I did and key findings:

I researched the Canaanite/Ugaritic pantheon via web search and authored 18 figures as a single JSON array conforming exactly to the schema. Every domain, faculty, and item carries its own citation, and each figure has figure-level `sources[]`. All `origin` values are `"canon"`; all citations are real attested texts (KTU tablet numbers, the epics, omen/ritual texts, and Philo of Byblos/Sanchuniathon via Eusebius). No modern coinages, no "not attested."

Coverage and tiers:
- Deities (13): El, Athirat/Asherah, Dagan, Baal-Hadad, Anat, Yam, Mot, Athtart/Astarte, Shapshu, Yarikh, Kothar-wa-Hasis, Resheph, the Kotharot.
- Divine-descent ladder: Kirta (demigod, son of El — KTU 1.14-1.16), Daniʾilu/Danel (demigod via Rephaim status), Aqhat (scion, son of Daniel), the Rephaim (demigod tier, deified dead — KTU 1.20-1.22).

Key authoring decisions / findings:
- Domains vs. faculties kept strictly distinct: domains = what they are god OF (e.g., Baal's `storm`/`kingship`); faculties = verb-phrase acts (e.g., "Smite Yam in single combat," Anat's "Cleave, winnow, and grind the death-god like grain").
- Native terms given as Ugaritic transliteration + romanization throughout (e.g., bʿl/baʿlu, nrt ʾilm/nayyiratu ʾilīma, btlt ʿnt).
- Baal's two Kothar-forged maces are both included: Yagrush and Ayamur (sources disagree on which is "Driver" vs "Chaser"; I assigned Chaser/Driver per the dominant reading and noted both as the pair from KTU 1.2 IV).
- Parent links wired within the batch where attested: Baal→Dagan; Anat, Mot, Yam, Shapshu→El; Aqhat→Daniel. El, Athirat, Dagan, Athtart, Yarikh, Kothar, Resheph, Kotharot, Rephaim have empty parentIds (no in-batch parent attested).
- vitalStatus reflects myth: Yam and Aqhat `dead`; Rephaim `dead` (the deified dead); Mot `living` (endures as the drought cycle); rest `living`.
- Typing the heroes honestly: Kirta = demigod (explicit "son of El"); Daniel = demigod (counted among the Rephaim, mt rpʾi); Aqhat = scion (son of the deified Daniel); Rephaim = demigod tier.
