Two judgment calls worth flagging: I added Itelmen Tylval (attested by Krasheninnikov) as a 24th figure to balance the thin Itelmen set and meet the ~24 target; and I parented Selkup Icha to Nom following the "later version" the sources record (the alternative orphan/Ilyntyl-Kota-grandmother tradition is preserved in his claims and notes).
