Summary: All ~113 faculties across the 60 Greek figures are termed with attested Greek vocabulary, no em-dashes required. Inheritability marked partial only for bodily/blood powers (Heraclid βίη strength across Hyllus/Telephus/Cleodaeus; Helen's κάλλος; Cycnus's ἄτρωτος invulnerability; Idmon's and Neleus's divinely-sired faculties; Periphetes's inherited χωλεία lameness from Hephaestus); none for all spheres, titles, and offices. Three signature powers were appended (Helen's εἴδωλον; and the rest were already covered). Key office-words used: βασιλεύς/ἄναξ/βασίλεια/βασίλισσα (kingship/queenship), διαρχία (Spartan dual kingship), οἰκιστής/ἀρχηγέτης/γενάρχης/πρόγονος (founding/progenitor), μάντις/μαντική/μαντεῖον (seercraft/oracle), καθαρμός (purification), ξενία-adjacent εὐσέβεια (piety), ἀριστεία (battlefield excellence), ὕβρις (overreach), πυγμή (boxing), θήρα (hunting).
