Notes on scope and decisions:

- **Counts:** 18 Aztec/Mexica, 15 Maya, 8 Inca = 41 new figures. None duplicate the existing keys you flagged.
- **Aztec id prefix:** I used `aztec_` to match the explicit `aztec_coatlicue` example in your spec and to keep parentId links resolvable, even though the three pre-existing Aztec entries (`mixcoatl`, `chimalman`, `topiltzin`) are bare. If you instead want bare ids, strip the `aztec_` prefix uniformly.
- **Parentage (in-corpus/this-batch only):** Huitzilopochtli/Coyolxauhqui → `aztec_coatlicue`; Tezcatlipoca/Quetzalcoatl/Xipe Totec → `aztec_ometeotl` (the four Tezcatlipocas born of the dual creator, per Histoyre du Mechique / Codex Ríos); Centeotl → `aztec_xochiquetzal` (Florentine Codex variant parentage); Pariacaca → `inca_cuniraya_viracocha` (Huarochirí). Deities with no canon parent get `[]`.
- **Mixtec:** I added none. The existing 10 (1 Deer creator couple, 9 Wind "Quetzalcoatl," 8 Deer, etc.) already cover the central attested Mixtec codex figures; there is no further *major* pan-Mixtec deity reliably attested by name beyond those, so adding more would risk fabrication.
- **Ometeotl caveat:** noted in its `notes` that "Ometeotl" is partly a modern scholarly synthesis (Léon-Portilla) over genuinely attested dual deities (Ometecuhtli/Omecihuatl = Tonacatecuhtli/Tonacacihuatl). It is retained because you listed it and the dual creator is well attested; flagging in case you prefer to drop the synthesized label.
- **Named items per your spec are all included and cited:** Tezcatlipoca's smoking mirror, Huitzilopochtli's Xiuhcoatl, Quetzalcoatl's wind-jewel (ehecacozcatl) and conch, Chaac's lightning-axe (baat), plus Coyolxauhqui's cheek-bells, Tlaloc's lightning staff, Illapa's sling, Mama Sara's maize-doll, etc.
- **Sources** are restricted to genuine primaries: Sahagún's Florentine Codex (by book), Leyenda de los Soles / Histoyre du Mechique / Codex Borgia-Borbonicus-Ríos (Aztec); Popol Vuh, Landa, and the Dresden/Madrid codices (Maya); Cobo, Garcilaso, Betanzos, Sarmiento, Polo de Ondegardo, González Holguín, and the Huarochirí Manuscript (Inca).
