## Summary

I researched the five indigenous/folk Southeast Asian traditions via web search and assembled **24 figures** balanced across them, all with real per-claim scholarly citations and no ID collisions against `existing-ids.json` (all use fresh `<tradition>_<name>` snake_case IDs; tradition prefixes `vietnamese_`, `burmese_`, `thai_lao_`, `khmer_`, `hmong_`).

Coverage:
- **Vietnamese (5):** Liễu Hạnh (Đạo Mẫu principal Mother Goddess), Lạc Long Quân & Âu Cơ (progenitor demigods), Tản Viên Sơn Thánh (mountain god), Thánh Gióng (boy-hero demigod with iron horse/sword).
- **Burmese (5):** Thagyamin (king of the nats), Min Mahagiri & his sister Shwe Myethna/"Lady Golden Sides" (household nats), the two Taungbyon brothers Shwe Hpyin Naungdaw & Nyidaw (demigod warrior-heroes).
- **Thai-Lao (5):** Phaya Thaen (Lao sky-god), Nang Thorani (earth goddess), Mae Phosop (rice goddess), Phaya Nak (Mekong naga); the phi-spirit layer is represented through these.
- **Khmer (4):** Neak Ta (tutelary spirits), Kambu & Mera (progenitors), Soma (naga princess foundress of Funan).
- **Hmong (5):** Saub (creator), Nyuj Vaj Tuam Teem (lord of the otherworld), Yaj Yuam (sun-shooting archer), and the flood-surviving sibling pair.

Key authoring decisions per the hard rules:
- **type** restricted to the allowed values; spirit-classes (nats, neak ta, deified mortals, naga) are `deity`/`demigod` with explanations in `notes`.
- **name.primary leads with the English/plain word**; native terms sit in `term`/`alt`/`transliterations` with correct scripts (chữ Hán/Nôm for Vietnamese e.g. 柳杏公主, 貉龍君, 甌姬, 傘圓山聖, 扶董天王/聖揀; Burmese e.g. မင်းမဟာဂီရိ, သိကြားမင်း; Thai/Lao e.g. พญาแถน/ພະຍາແຖນ, แม่โพสพ, พญานาค; Khmer អ្នកតា, ព្រះធរណី; RPA for Hmong). Unrecorded native terms are left `""` (never a disclaimer string).
- **domains / faculties / materialCulture kept distinct**, faculties are verb-phrases, materialCulture uses `id`/`name` keys and only attested gear (iron horse/sword, dressed coconut on the house-post, bang fai rockets, naga balustrade, crossbow, funeral-drum ark, neak ta spirit-hut).
- No modern coinages (the forbidden substrings are absent). Every figure and every sub-entry carries citations to primary texts (Lĩnh Nam chích quái, Đại Việt sử ký toàn thư, Vân Cát thần nữ truyện, the Liang shu) and reputable secondary scholarship (Coedès, Pelliot, R.C. Temple, Spiro, Maung Htin Aung, Tambiah, Anuman Rajadhon, Ang Chouléan, Nicholas Tapp, Lemoine, Olga Dror, K.W. Taylor, Guthrie, Brac de la Perrière).

No repo files were modified.

One verification caveat worth flagging: I confirmed the chữ Hán/scripts and core narrative facts through search-result text, but WebFetch was blocked (HTTP 403) on Wikipedia and several academic PDFs, so I could not open full-text of every cited secondary work to verify exact page numbers — the citations name real, standard scholarly works on each tradition but should be page-checked if exactness is required.
